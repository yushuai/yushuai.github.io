Biography

I am an Assistant Professor in the School of Computer Science and Technology at Donghua University. I received my Ph.D. degree from Fudan University. I have published over 20 papers in top conferences and journals. Additionally, I serve as an area chair at ICASSP 2025. I also serve as a reviewer for several top journals and conferences, including TASLP, TMM, TAC, AAAI, ACM MM, ICASSP, ICME. My main research areas include Music AI, cross-modal information processing and retrieval.

If you are a DHU student interested in doing research with me, please feel free to drop me an email.
